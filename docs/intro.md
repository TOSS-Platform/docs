# Welcome to TOSS Protocol

**TOSS** is a next-generation decentralized fund platform that rebuilds asset management using blockchain transparency, automated risk controls, and zero-knowledge scalability.

## What is TOSS?

TOSS Protocol enables **permissionless, transparent, risk-validated fund management** on zkSync Era:

- **Anyone can become a Fund Manager** backed by mandatory TOSS staking
- **Investors gain access** to transparently managed, diversified crypto funds
- **Every action is validated** by an autonomous RiskEngine
- **Accountability enforced** through automatic slashing mechanisms
- **Built on zkSync** for ultra-low fees and native Account Abstraction

## Key Features

### Transparent Fund Management
All trades, NAV updates, risk parameters, and fund performance are publicly verifiable on-chain.

### Autonomous RiskEngine
Every trade is validated against mathematical risk boundaries before executionâ€”no human intervention needed.

### Economic Security
Fund Managers stake TOSS tokens as collateral. Violations trigger automatic slashing and burning.

### zkSync Integration
Ultra-low transaction costs (under $0.10) enable real-time risk validation and frequent operations.

### Multi-Level Governance
Three governance levels (Fund, FM, Protocol) ensure relevant stakeholders make relevant decisions with context-appropriate voting power.
