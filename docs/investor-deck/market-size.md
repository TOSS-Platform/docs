# Market Opportunity

## Total Addressable Market (TAM)

**$4.4 Trillion** - Global Asset Management Industry

- Hedge Funds: $4.4T AUM globally
- Private Equity: $6.3T AUM
- Total Investment Management: $100T+

## Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)

**$50 Billion** - Crypto-Native Investment Products

- Crypto Hedge Funds: ~$40B AUM (200+ funds)
- DeFi TVL: $50B+ (potential for active management)
- Institutional Crypto Allocations: $10B+ (growing rapidly)

## Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)

**$5 Billion AUM by Year 3** (10% of SAM, 0.1% of TAM)

Conservative market capture assumptions:
- 500 active funds
- Average fund size: $10M
- 50,000 retail + institutional investors
