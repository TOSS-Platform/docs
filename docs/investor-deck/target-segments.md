# Target Segments

**Size**: 10,000+ experienced crypto traders  
**Pain Point**: No infrastructure to launch professional funds  
**Value Prop**: Launch fund in minutes, proven track record on-chain

### 2. TradFi Managers Entering Crypto

**Size**: 5,000+ traditional hedge fund professionals  
**Pain Point**: Need transparent infrastructure for crypto exposure  
**Value Prop**: Familiar fund structure with blockchain advantages

### 3. Retail Crypto Investors

**Size**: 50M+ crypto holders globally  
**Pain Point**: Lack access to professional management  
**Value Prop**: Invest in quality funds with $100+ minimums

### 4. Institutional Investors

**Size**: 1,000+ family offices, endowments  
**Pain Point**: Require transparent, auditable crypto exposure  
**Value Prop**: Enterprise-grade compliance and reporting
