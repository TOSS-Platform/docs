# Go-to-Market Strategy

## Phase 1: Crypto-Native Launch (Months 1-3)

**Target**: Experienced crypto traders with track records

**Strategy**:
- Hand-pick 10-20 FMs from crypto Twitter, Discord
- Offer reduced fees for early adopters
- Intensive product feedback loop
- Goal: $10-50M AUM

## Phase 2: Community Expansion (Months 4-12)

**Target**: Broader crypto community

**Strategy**:
- FM education program & certification
- Integration with portfolio trackers (Zapper, DeBank)
- Community-driven fund discovery
- Goal: 50-100 funds, $100-500M AUM

## Phase 3: Institutional Onboarding (Year 2)

**Target**: TradFi managers, family offices

**Strategy**:
- White-glove onboarding
- Compliance features (KYC/AML)
- Prime broker partnerships
- Goal: $1-5B AUM

## Phase 4: Mass Market (Year 3+)

**Target**: Global retail investors

**Strategy**:
- Mobile app launch
- Simplified UX
- Localization (10+ languages)
- Goal: $5B+ AUM
