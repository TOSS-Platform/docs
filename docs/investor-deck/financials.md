## Key Assumptions

- Average fund size: $2M (Y1) → $5M (Y2) → $10M (Y3)
- Protocol fee: 0.1% AUM annually
- Performance cut: 2% of FM fees
- FM fee average: 15% of profits
- Churn rate: 10% annually
