# Best Practices

## Overview

Recommended best practices for using TOSS effectively and safely.

## Fund Management

### Fund Creation

- **Choose Appropriate Risk Tier**: Match risk tier to strategy
- **Set Realistic Limits**: Don't set limits too tight or too loose
- **Plan Stake Requirements**: Ensure sufficient stake before launching
- **Test Configuration**: Validate config before deployment

### Fund Operations

- **Monitor Risk Metrics**: Check risk limits regularly
- **Stay Within Limits**: Respect all risk boundaries
- **Track Performance**: Monitor NAV and performance consistently
- **Adjust Strategy**: Adapt to market conditions

## Investment Strategy

### Diversification

- **Don't Over-Concentrate**: Spread risk across assets
- **Balance Portfolio**: Maintain healthy asset allocation
- **Monitor Correlation**: Watch for correlated risks

### Risk Management

- **Understand Limits**: Know your fund's risk limits
- **Plan for Drawdowns**: Prepare for market downturns
- **Use Stop-Loss**: Consider implementing stop-loss strategies

## Security

### Wallet Management

- Use hardware wallets for large amounts
- Keep private keys secure and offline
- Use session keys for trading
- Regular security audits

### Access Control

- Limit permissions to minimum necessary
- Rotate keys regularly
- Monitor all operations
- Report suspicious activity

## Performance Optimization

### Fee Management

- Understand fee structures
- Monitor fee impact on returns
- Optimize fund size for efficiency

### Tax Considerations

- Track all transactions
- Maintain accurate records
- Consult tax professionals
- Understand jurisdiction requirements
