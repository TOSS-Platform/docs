# Analytics & Reporting

## Overview

Understanding analytics and performance reporting in TOSS.

## Performance Analytics

### Key Performance Indicators

- **Total Return**: Percentage gain/loss
- **Sharpe Ratio**: Risk-adjusted return
- **Maximum Drawdown**: Largest peak-to-trough decline
- **Volatility**: Standard deviation of returns

### Performance Reports

Generate reports for:
- Daily performance
- Weekly summary
- Monthly report
- Quarterly analysis
- Annual review

## Fee Analytics

### Fee Breakdown

View detailed fee information:
- Management fees collected
- Performance fees earned
- Withdrawal fees paid
- Total fees over time

### Fee Impact Analysis

Understand how fees affect returns:
- Net returns after fees
- Fee percentage of gains
- Comparison to industry benchmarks

## Risk Analytics

### Risk Metrics

Monitor risk exposure:
- Position concentration
- Asset allocation risk
- Leverage exposure
- Drawdown risk

### Risk Alerts

Receive alerts for:
- Risk limit breaches
- High concentration
- Unusual volatility
- Significant drawdowns
